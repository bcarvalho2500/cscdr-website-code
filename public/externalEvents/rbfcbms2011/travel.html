NSF-CBMS Regional Research Conferences in the Mathematical Sciences

June 20 - 24, 2011, UMass Dartmouth, Dartmouth, MA, USA

Lecture Place

Conference and lectures will be held at UMass Dartmouth Advanced Technology and Manufacturing Center. The center is about 10 minute drive from UMass Dartmouth. The address is: 151 Martine Street Fall River, MA 02723. Directions can be found here.

Lodging Information

There are 2 choices of lodging schemes.

  • Stay in university dormitory rooms

    This is following classical tradition of NSF-CBMS conferences. Conference participants will stay at Willow Hall dormitory. Check the location of Willow Hall in UMass Dartmouth map of dormitory complexes.
    • Check in: Sunday, June 19 2011. Starting at 4:00 pm
    • Place of check in: Lobby of Willow Hall
    • Parking: Parking lot 10.
    • Organizers in charge: Saeja Kim, Cheng Wang, and Brandi Ruiz (summer housing liason).
    • Check out: Friday, June 24 2011. Starting at 2:00 pm

    If you arrive at the dorm late or early, please contact the (24/7) on call duty official. Name: Paul. Contact numbers (508)246-3189 (cell) and (774)929-2492 (room).

    The linen package that includes sheets, pillow, pillowcase, towels and a blanket will be provided. Cleaning supplies are available in each apartment but there is no cleaning service.

    Bus from dormitory complex to conference place

    Daily round-trip transportation from university dorm to conference place (ATMC) will be provided. The buses will pick participants up right outside of the campus center where breakfast will be served each day. (The main doors to the campus center).

  • Hotel

    Several hotels around Dartmouth and Fall River are within short driving distance to the conference center. This option is for participants who want flexibility in choosing their own lodging. The closest hotel to our conference site is Hampton Inn Fall River-Westport. The hotel address is 53 Old Bedford Road, Westport, Massachusetts, USA 02790. Participants should use Group/Convention code ATM when reserving on-line by visiting www.HamptonInn.com or by calling 1-800-HAMPTON.

Internet Access

  • University dormitory rooms

    Dormitory rooms do not have wireless internet access. However, Ethernet ports are available. Participants need to register Media Access Control (MAC) address of their laptops. Please fill this form and send it by e-mail to both Saeja Kim and Alfa Heryudono. Please also bring your own network (ethernet) cable.

  • Conference rooms

    Wireless internet access is available in conference rooms.
