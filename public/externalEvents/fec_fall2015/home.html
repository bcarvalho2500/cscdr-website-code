Accommodations & Directions

Accommodations
  • Residence Inn Marriott has a special room rate for the conference participants: $115 for a studio suite and $119 for a one bedroom suite.
  • A block of rooms are held for three nights from Thursday to Saturday, and will be released on 9/22/2015.
  • The rate includes full hot breakfast buffet daily, wireless internet, and 24 hour access to the hotel fitness center.
  • How to make hotel reservations
    • Call the hotel at 508-984-5858 and request the group rate for UMass Finite Element Circus 2015.
    • Visit the Residence Inn Dartmouth website , choose the dates for the group, and under special rates go down to the group code area. The codes are listed below:
      • UMFUMFA is the code for the studio suite at $115.
      • UMFUMFC is the code for the one bedroom suite at $119.
  • Lunch on Friday will be provided from 12:30pm to 1:30pm at the conference venue. Lunch on Saturday will be provided too as usual.
