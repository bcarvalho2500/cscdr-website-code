Selected attractions in the area
  • Fall foliage should be around its peak time in MA and NH.
    • The White Mountain area of New Hampshire is 3 hours (driving) away.
    • Maine is also a frequent go-to place for fall foliage. It is about two more hours away than the White Mountain area.

  • 18 miles away from the conference venue, known as a New England summer resort and famous for its mansions, Newport, RI is a very popular destination in the area.

  • Cape Cod destinations are within 1 to 2 hours away.

  • If you are a beach person and the weather allows, a major beach on the southeast coast, the horseneck beach is 14 miles away from the conference venue.
